Afghan proverbs carry wisdom passed down through generations. Discover them and learn their meanings.

Brother to brother, accounts are balanced.

Between brothers, accounts are balanced.

One hand cannot clap alone.

You need cooperation to succeed.

A crooked load never reaches its destination.

Bar Kij be manzel nemiresad!

Dishonest or wrong actions never succeed or reach their goal.

Too many cooks spoil the broth.

When there are two cooks, the soup becomes either too salty or tasteless.

Too many people managing something causes confusion and failure.

Still water stinks.

Aab ke yekja bimanad, migandad.

Stagnation causes decay or problems.

Good intention brings good result.

Niyat-e khoob, natije-ye khoob.

A good intention leads to a good outcome.
